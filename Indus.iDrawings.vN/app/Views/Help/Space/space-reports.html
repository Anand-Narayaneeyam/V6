Space reports are available under Reports icon.

The reports generated by the system can be viewed using the controls available in the report viewer. The report can be saved to Excel, PDF or RTF format by clicking on Excel, PDF or RTF button. The following reports are available in Space module.

Gross Area Distribution

The Gross Area Distribution Report gives the distribution of space areas among building common, floor common, usable area, chargeable area for the selected floors.

Gross Area Organizational Distribution

This Report gives detailed information on the Space Assigned (usable gross, common area, chargeable area) to various organizational units (Divisions, Cost Centers etc.).Text reports and graphical reports with pie chart and bar chart are also available. The chargeable area in this report depends on the option ‘Org. Units Assignment to Common Area’ settings done by Indus. If this option is enabled, the area distribution can be set either to Assigned Organizational units or to All Organizational Units. By default the Floor Common Area is distributed to all organizational units in the floor and the Building Common and Vertical area is distributed to all organizational units in the building.The cost column is visible only if the ‘Enable Cost Category’ option is enabled by Indus.

If there are spaces which are shared among different Organizational Units, the Usable Gross, Common Area, and Chargeable Area for those spaces will be calculated according to the percentage of sharing.

Space Details

This Report gives the space details (Space key, Space function, Gross Area, Net Area etc) of the selected floors.

Net Area Distribution

The Net Area Distribution Report gives the distribution of Net Area for the selected floors.

Net Area Organizational Distribution

This Report gives the distribution of Net Area across various organizational units (Divisions, Cost Centers etc.). The Net Area related Reports are only for Accounts with Net Area drawings that have this option enabled by Indus.

Sites

This report shows the list of all sites, with site name, site code, building count and status.

Buildings

This report shows the list of all buildings, with site name, building name, building details and floor count.

Floors

This report shows the list of all floors, with site name, building name and floor name.

Users

This report shows the list of all Users. This report can be filtered by selecting the criteria like deleted users, active users, blocked users, etc. from the list.

Space Functions

This report shows the list of all Space Functions. This will be available only if the ‘Enable Space Function option is enabled by Indus.

Gross Area Report

The Gross Area Report gives the Site wise or Building wise or Floor wise Gross Area and Net Area for the selected Sites/Buildings/Floors.

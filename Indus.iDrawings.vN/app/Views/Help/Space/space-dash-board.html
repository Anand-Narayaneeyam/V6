On clicking Space module icon, a Dashboard page will be displayed with a visual indication of the Gross Area Distribution, Gross Area Organizational Distribution and Building wise Area Distribution by a graphical representation of Pie Charts and Bar chart respectively.

Area in Sq. Ft. is displayed as a bar chart which represents Usable Gross Area, Common Area and Chargeable Gross Area in each building. Gross Area Distribution and Organizational Distribution are represented as Pie Charts where the Gross Area Distribution shows the area distributed among Usable Space, External Wall, Vertical Space, Floor Common and Building Common as Gross Area Organizational Distribution displays the Area distribution among various Organizational Units. Corresponding Legends are also provided in each chart.

A Text Report of Organizational Distribution is also provided in the Dashboard which lists all the assigned Organizational Units along with its total Gross Area and Net Area in the decreasing Order of Area. Spaces with no Organizational Units assigned are grouped under Unassigned and is listed with Organizational Units.

Dashboard is available for users with Administrator privilege only.

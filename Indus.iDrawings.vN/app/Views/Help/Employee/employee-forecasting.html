Seating Capacities by Organizational Unit over Time

This option is to show the Trend and Forecasting of Total Seating Capacity of an Organizational Unit based on the Snapshots created. On clicking Seating Capacities by Organizational Unit over Time menu, a period, Organizational Unit selection window will be displayed.

Show Trend

Select the required period and Organizational Unit, click icon. A Line graph will be displayed with the selected Organizational Unit’s trend in the selected period based on the Snap Shot created.

Show Forecast

This option is to view forecast for 3 years by entering a Growth Rate.

Select the required period and Organizational Unit, click the Show Forecast icon. Enter Growth Rate (%) and click OK button, a graph will be displayed with Year in X-axis and Seating Capacity in Y-axis. Line Graphs of Total Seating Capacity (Green color) and the Seating Capacity of the selected Organizational Unit (Red color) will be displayed with respect to the selected Years.

Occupancy by Grade over Time

This option is to show the Trend and Forecasting of total number of employees in each grade during a year based on the snapshot created for a time period. On clicking Occupancy by Grade over Time menu, a period selection window will be displayed.

Show Trend

Select the required period and Grades, click Show Trend icon. A Line graph will be displayed showing the trend of the selected Grades in the selected period based on the Snap Shot created.

Show Forecast

This option is to view forecast for 3 years by entering a Growth Rate.

Select the required period and Grades, and click the Show Forecast icon. Enter Growth Rate (%) and click OK button, a graph will be displayed with Year in X-axis and Number of Employees in Y-axis. Line Graphs of Total Employees in all grades and Total Employees in the selected Grades based on the selected year will be displayed.

Hiring Rate over Time

This option is to show the Trend and Forecasting of Employees hiring rate based on the Employee Organizational Unit. On clicking Hiring Rate over Time menu, a period selection window will be displayed.

Show Trend

Select the required period and Organizational Unit, click Show Trend icon. A Line graph will be displayed showing the trend of the Employee Hiring Rate in the selected period for the selected Employee Organizational Unit.

Show Forecast

This option is to view forecast for 3 years by entering a Growth Rate.

Select the required period and Organizational Unit, and click the Show Forecast icon. Enter Growth Rate (%) and click OK button, a graph will be displayed with Year in X-axis and Number of Employees in Y-axis. Line Graphs of Total Employees hired (Green color) and the number of Employees hired in the selected Organizational Unit (Red color) based on the selected year will be displayed.

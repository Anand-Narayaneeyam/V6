On clicking the Furniture module icon a Dash board page will be displayed with pie charts of Furniture Distribution by Furniture Class and Furniture Distribution by Organizational Unit.

A List of Furniture Distribution by Floor and Furniture List by Floor are also provided in the Dashboard which lists the distribution of all the Furniture assigned in the floor.

Furniture Distribution by Furniture Class pie chart shows the Class wise distribution of all the Furniture. The pie chart tooltip shows the percentage and number of Furniture in each Furniture Class. The legend displays the top 9 Furniture Classes. All other Furniture in various Furniture Classes are summed up and shown as a single item labeled as 'Others'.

Furniture Distribution by Organizational Unit pie chart shows the Organizational Unit (Division, Cost Center, etc) wise distribution of the Furniture. The pie chart tooltip shows the percentage of Furniture and number of Furniture in each Organizational Unit. The legend displays the top 9 Organizational Units according to the distribution. All other Furniture in the remaining Organizational Units are summed up and shown as a single item labeled as 'Others'.

Furniture Distribution by Floor displays the total number Furniture located in each floor, and Furniture List by Floor displays the class wise total number of Furniture located in each floor.

Dashboard is available only for users with Administrator privilege.
